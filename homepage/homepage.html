Recomended places to visit

Unity Park

The Unity Park of Ethiopia is one of the most modern and multi-faceted tourist attractions in the country. The Ethiopian Unity Park is in the compound of the National Grand Palace, which is at Arat kilo in Addis Ababa.

Lake Tana

Often the first stop along Ethiopia's northern circuit, the lakeside town of Bahir Dar is your launchpad onto Lake Tana. Flamingos and hippos roam these waters, and ancient monasteries await discovery on the islands dotted around the lake.

Omo National Park

The Omo National Park - one of the Ethiopia's largest and richest nature sanctuary and yet one of the least visited areas in East and Central Africa.

The Jugol Wall

Believed to be built somewhere between the 13th and 16th century, this thick, 5 metre high and 3.5km long wall was built as a fortress surrounding the ancient city.

Rockhewn Churches of Lalibela

The 11 medieval monolithic cave churches of this 13th-century 'New Jerusalem' are situated in a mountainous region in the heart of Ethiopia near a traditional village with circular-shaped dwellings.

Axum Oblisek

Occupying prime position in the Axum skyline, the Obelisks of Axum have a history steeped in war, power, occupation and hope.
